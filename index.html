Dragon Ball Super

Dragon Ball Super is a continuation of the series Dragon Ball Z Buu's Saga. This series has some new adventures and new challenges for Goku and his friends. Goku met some new fighters from other unvierses.

Main Characters

Goku

Goku is the main character of the series. To let you know, he has died a lot of times and ressurected a lot of times like seriously. He also have saved he world a lot of times already. He is the strongest man in his universe second to the god of destruction.

Gohan

He is the son of Goku. He did also save the world once by beating cell. He is an intelligent student of a cool school.Now he is trining for the universal tournament.

Vegeta

Vegeta

Vegeta is the biggest rival of Goku. He can match Goku's power but not right now.

Trunks and Goten

Trunks is the son of Vegeta. Goten is the son of Goku. Both can fuse into one stronger hero which is Gotenks.
